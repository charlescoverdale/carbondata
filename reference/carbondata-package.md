# carbondata: Access Carbon Market Data from Emissions Trading Systems and Voluntary Registries

Unified access to carbon market data from compliance emissions trading
systems ('EU ETS', 'UK ETS', 'RGGI', California Cap-and-Trade) and
voluntary carbon markets (Verra, Gold Standard, American Carbon
Registry, Climate Action Reserve, via the Berkeley Voluntary Registry
Offsets Database and the 'CarbonPlan' 'OffsetsDB' API). Includes
cross-market price data from the 'International Carbon Action
Partnership' ('ICAP') Allowance Price Explorer
<https://icapcarbonaction.com/en/ets-prices>, global carbon pricing from
the World Bank Carbon Pricing Dashboard
<https://carbonpricingdashboard.worldbank.org/>, and the historical
'RFF' World Carbon Pricing Database following Dolphin, Pollitt and
Newbery (2020)
[doi:10.1038/s41597-022-01659-x](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-022-01659-x)
. Data is downloaded from public sources on first use and cached
locally.
